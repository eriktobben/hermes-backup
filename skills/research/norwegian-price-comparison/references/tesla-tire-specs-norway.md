## Minimum requirements for Tesla Model 3

- **Load index**: Minimum 96 (for 2019-2021 models). Check owner's manual for exact spec.
- **Speed rating**: Minimum W (270 km/h) recommended for Performance models
- **Tire type**: Must be XL (Extra Load) for EVs due to vehicle weight (~1,800 kg)
- **Season**: Norwegian law requires winter tires (with M+S or snowflake symbol) October-April in many areas

## EV-specific considerations

1. **Tire wear**: EVs weigh 200-300 kg more than ICE equivalents → faster tire wear
2. **Noise**: Look for tires with low dB rating (EV cabin is quiet, tire noise more noticeable)
3. **Rolling resistance**: Lower = more range. EU label A/B rating preferred
4. **Torque**: EV instant torque wears tires faster, especially rear axle
5. **Recommended rotation**: Every 8,000-10,000 km for EVs (vs. 15,000 for ICE)
