## Key Observations for Creative Workloads

- **36 GB is INSUFFICIENT for 1 GB+ PSD files with many layers.** The 14-core M4 Max is locked to 36 GB, making it unsuitable for heavy Adobe work despite the attractive entry price.
- **64 GB is the minimum viable** for serious Photoshop work with files >500 MB.
- **96 GB is the sweet spot** when working with multi-GB files + multiple CC apps simultaneously.
- **M3 Ultra's 819 GB/s bandwidth** makes a real difference when opening/saving large files because the file operations hit memory bandwidth limits on M4 Max.
- **Front ports differ**: M4 Max has USB-C (10 Gbps) on front; M3 Ultra has Thunderbolt 5 (120 Gbps) on front — better for connecting fast external SSDs.
- **Weight matters**: M4 Max 2.74 kg, M3 Ultra 3.64 kg.
