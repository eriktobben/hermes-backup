# Tesla software & subscription pricing (Norway, 2025/2026)

Current prices for **Model 3/Y** sold in Norway. Updated per Tesla support pages and third-party sources (April 2026).

## What's included (no extra cost)

| Feature | Notes |
|:--------|:------|
| **Basic Autopilot** | Adaptive cruise control + Autosteer (lane keeping). Included on all 2021+ cars sold in Europe. Grandfathered — not affected by May 2026 subscription change for new cars. |
| **Standard Connectivity** | Navigation (basic routing), software updates, remote app control (lock/unlock, climate pre-conditioning). |
| **Sentry Mode / Dashcam** | Records on a USB drive in the glovebox. No connectivity costs. |
| **OTA updates** | All software improvements (bug fixes, new features, range increases). |

## Optional subscriptions

| Service | Price | What you get |
|:--------|:-----:|:-------------|
| **Premium Connectivity** | **129 kr/mnd** | Live traffic overlay, satellite map view, car internet browser, Netflix/YouTube/Spotify streaming in the car, Sentry Mode live remote view. Can buy via touchscreen or Tesla app — no binding period. |
| **FSD (Supervised)** — without EAP | **~99 €/mnd** ≈ **1.150 kr/mnd** | Full self-driving: auto lane change, navigate on Autopilot (highway), Summon / Smart Summon, autopark, traffic light & stop sign control. *Driver must supervise at all times.* |
| **FSD (Supervised)** — with EAP | **~49 €/mnd** ≈ **570 kr/mnd** | Same as above but discounted if you already bought Enhanced Autopilot. |

## One-time purchases (may be phased out)

| Feature | Price (approx.) | Notes |
|:--------|:---------------:|:------|
| **Acceleration Boost** (LR AWD only) | ~3.500 kr | 0-100 km/h from 4,4 s → 3,9 s. No subscription — permanent. |
| **Enhanced Autopilot (EAP)** | ~3.500-4.500 € (historisk) | Auto lane change, Navigate on Autopilot, Summon, Autopark. May be discontinued for new purchases in Europe — check current configurator. |

## Important: May 2026 change

As of May 2026, Tesla **stopped selling EAP and FSD as one-time purchases** in Europe — they became subscription-only. However:
- **Existing cars** that already have Basic Autopilot included → keep it. No change.
- **Used car buyers** (like a 2021 Model 3) → the car's original Autopilot configuration transfers with the car. No forced subscription.
- **New car buyers** after May 2026 → may need to subscribe to get Autosteer.

**Bottom line for used Model 3 buyers**: You get Basic Autopilot permanently, no subscription needed. Premium Connectivity is the only optional subscription most owners actually use (129 kr/mnd).

## Cost comparison for home vs Supercharger vs petrol

Assumptions: 1.800 Wh/mil consumption (Model 3 LR AWD mixed winter/summer), or 0,65 L/mil for a compact petrol car.

| Scenario | Cost per mil | KRS-Oslo (330 km) one way | 15.000 km/år |
|:---------|:-----------:|:-------------------------:|:------------:|
| 🏠 **Tesla — home charging** (1 kr/kWh) | ~1,80 kr | ~60 kr | ~2.700 kr |
| ⚡ **Tesla — Supercharger** (4,50 kr/kWh) | ~8,10 kr | ~270 kr | ~12.150 kr |
| ⚡ **Tesla — Supercharger w/ membership** (3 kr/kWh) | ~5,40 kr | ~180 kr | ~8.100 kr |
| ⚡ **Tesla — Ionity/other fast** (6 kr/kWh) | ~10,80 kr | ~360 kr | ~16.200 kr |
| ⛽ **Petrol car** (21 kr/L, 0,65 L/mil) | ~13,65 kr | ~450 kr | ~20.500 kr |

**Supercharger vs other fast chargers**: Tesla Supercharger is typically 20-50% cheaper per kWh than competitors (Ionity, Recharge, Circle K, Mer) in Norway. The gap has widened as Tesla continues to operate at lower margin on charging.
