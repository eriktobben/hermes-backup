# Estimated cosmetic repair costs — Norwegian body shops (2025–2026)

Typical workshop prices in the Oslo / Eastern Norway area. Prices include VAT (mva) but are rough estimates — actual quotes vary by workshop, paint brand, and extent of damage.

## Bodywork / Lakk (per panel)

| Issue | Estimated cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Steinsprut i frontrute** — reparasjon | 800–1 500 kr | Small chips/dings, if repairable (not in driver's line of sight) |
| **Steinsprut i frontrute** — skift | 8 000–15 000 kr | Full windshield replacement. Includes calibration of cameras/sensors for modern cars (Tesla, etc.) |
| **Riper i lakk** — polering (single panel) | 500–1 500 kr | Only for light surface scratches that don't reach primer |
| **Riper i lakk** — lokallakkering (single panel) | 2 000–4 000 kr | Scratches through clear coat and paint, but no dent |
| **Helt lakkering av panel** (dør, skjerm, etc.) | 4 000–7 000 kr | Full panel respray. Price depends on color complexity (pearl/metallic vs solid) |
| **Bulk uten lakkskade** — bankes/rettes | 2 000–4 000 kr | PDR (Paintless Dent Repair) for small-medium dents |
| **Bulk med lakkskade** — rettes + lakk | 3 000–6 000 kr | Small to medium dent that needs filler + paint |
| **Støtfanger foran/bak** — lakkering | 4 000–6 000 kr | Plastic bumper repair + paint |
| **Panser** — lakkering | 5 000–8 000 kr | Large panel, often needs blending into fenders |
| **Speilhus** — lakkering (per side) | 1 000–2 000 kr | Small part, quick job |
| **Dørtrekk** — reparasjon/nytt | 2 000–4 000 kr | Interior door panel — depends on availability of replacement trim piece |
| **Solfilm** — ny på en rute | 500–1 000 kr | Remove old + apply new tint film |
| **Taktrekk** — rens/damp | 500–1 000 kr | Professional interior shampoo of headliner |
| **Terskler/innsteg** — polering/reparasjon | 500–2 000 kr | Depending on severity — often plastic trim that gets scuffed |

## What's normal for a car at various km levels

| Km-stand | Expected cosmetic state | What to budget |
|---|---|---|
| **0–30 000 km** | Minimal wear. Occasional stone chip on hood. | 0–3 000 kr |
| **30 000–60 000 km** | Light stone chips on front, minor curb rash, light interior wear. | 3 000–8 000 kr |
| **60 000–100 000 km** | Noticeable stone chips, scratches from daily use, curb rash on 1–3 wheels. | 5 000–15 000 kr |
| **100 000–150 000 km** | Stone chips everywhere on front, scratches on multiple panels, curb rash all wheels, interior wear (seat bolsters, steering wheel). | 8 000–25 000 kr for "decent" condition |
| **150 000+ km** | At this point cosmetic perfection is not expected. Focus on mechanical. | 5 000–10 000 kr for the most visible issues only |

## When to negotiate vs fix yourself

- **Fix yourself** if: you care about perfection, have a trusted workshop, and want it done your way
- **Negotiate discount** if: the issues are cosmetic only (dealers are less motivated to discount for purely cosmetic wear)
- **Ask dealer to fix before delivery** if: the issues are mechanical/safety (front windshield, suspension, brakes) — dealers will often fix these to close the sale
- **Don't bother** with: small stone chips on a 100k+ km car, minor interior wear, light curb rash — this is normal wear that doesn't affect value or driving experience
